BasicGame
    SpriteSet       
		obstacle > Missile orientation=LEFT  speed=0.066 color=BROWN img=log
		avatar > MovingAvatar img=frog
		goal > Immovable img=coin
		wall > Immovable color=BLACK img=wall
		
    LevelMapping
        g > goal
        a > avatar
        w > wall
        l > obstacle
        
    InteractionSet
        goal avatar  > killSprite scoreChange=1
        avatar wall  > stepBack
        obstacle EOS > killSprite
        avatar obstacle > pullWithIt
    
    TerminationSet        
        SpriteCounter stype=goal limit=0 win=True
